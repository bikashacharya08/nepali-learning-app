Quick Grammar & Travel Tips

  • Sentence Structure: Nepali uses SOV (Subject - Object - Verb). E.g., "I apple eat" instead of "I eat an apple".
  • Politeness: Add "Hajur" to sound polite (e.g. Yes/Pardon?). Use "Tapai" for formal "You".
  • Greetings: "Namaste" is the universal greeting. Press your palms together at your chest.
  • Bargaining: In markets, it's common to bargain politely. Use "Ali milauhos na" (Please discount a little).
